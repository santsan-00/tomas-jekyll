Have you ever stopped mid viewing to look up something related to what you were watching?

Research and Brainstorm

“I want to be able to learn more about a movie,  to immerse myself in these stories and the artists  who brought them to life. These materials exist and  are put out on disc releases, so why can’t I enjoy them  on streaming platforms?” inputmag.com article

Problem Statement

Users want to know more about the content they’re consuming, but they have to leave the platform to get that information.

Our Oppurtunity

To offer the users of Netflix a centralised experience within the platform where they can get more information and context on the content they are consuming.

Testing Solutions

To understand how the feature would be used and received, we protoyped a flow mimicking the viewing experience and shared it with users.
